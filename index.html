Step-by-Step Installation: Get Started with Ease

Getting started with Live is straightforward. Here’s a quick guide to set it up on your computer:

1. Go to ledger.com/start from your browser

2. Choose your operating system: Windows, Mac, or Linux

3. Download and run the installer

4. Plug in your Ledger device using a USB cable

5. Open the app and select "Get Started"

6. Choose your device and click "Initialize" or "Restore"

7. Set a PIN and back up your recovery phrase

8. Complete the steps and access your dashboard

This process ensures that the app is securely tied to your hardware device, forming a safe digital boundary that only you can control.

Can Ledger Live Be Used Without a Hardware Device?

No, Live is not a standalone wallet or software-only application. It requires pairing with a Ledger device at all times. This ensures that all actions are validated on the secure hardware level and never leave the encrypted environment of your wallet.

This tight integration makes the system nearly impenetrable to remote threats, as nothing sensitive is ever exposed online or stored on your desktop or mobile system.

How to Restore Access If You Lose the App or Device

If you happen to lose your device or uninstall the app, you can restore access easily—so long as you still have your recovery phrase. Here’s what to do:

  • 1. Set up a new Ledger hardware device
  • 2. Download Live from ledger.com/start
  • 3. Choose the “Restore device” option
  • 4. Enter your 24-word recovery phrase
  • 5. Complete the setup and regain full access to your accounts

Never share or digitally store your recovery phrase. Keeping it offline and secure ensures you always have a backup route without sacrificing your safety.
